About Myself

My Name is Graham Kaye. I am currently enrolled in the Communication Design Diploma program at North Island College and will be graduating in the Summer of 2022.

I have two previous Bachelor of fine art degrees in painting/drawing (Emily Carr University, 2001), and Electroacoustic studies (Concordia, 2009).

My work history is long and varied, ranging from many years as a treeplanter / forman, carpentry, oyster farming and in art galleries trending to collections and installing art exhibits.

In my spare time I like to work on personal design related projects, paint, make music and garden. Whenever time allows it I like to get out to the coast and surf, something I am terrible at but love to do.
